# Northwind-Dataset-visualization Using Tableau
Northwind Sales Analysis & Tableau Dashboard

Project Overview:
This project presents an end-to-end data analysis and visualization of the Northwind dataset using Tableau. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making across products, customers, regions, and sales performance.

The workflow includes data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and interactive dashboard creation, focusing on uncovering patterns and trends in sales data.

Tools & Technologies:
Tableau (Dashboard & Story Design).
Python (Data validation & exploration).
SQL / CSV Dataset (Northwind).

Dataset:
The Northwind dataset is a sample database provided by Microsoft that contains transactional sales data, including:

Orders & Order Details
Customers
Products
Employees

📌 Source:
https://github.com/jpwhite3/northwind-SQLite3

Data Preparation:
Cleaned and structured raw data for analysis
Ensured consistency across joins (Orders, Products, Customers)
Created calculated fields such as:
Total Sales = Unit Price × Quantity
Handled aggregation issues and ensured accurate metrics

Dashboards Created
1. Northwind Global Sales Overview
Global Sales Distribution by Country (Map)
Monthly Sales Trend (Time Series)
Top 10 Products by Total Sales
Top Customers by Sales

2. Sales Performance & Pricing Insights
Sales Performance by Employee
Product Price vs Total Sales (Scatter/Bubble)
Cross-Tab Summary (Country vs Year)

Key Insights:
A small number of products generate a large share of revenue
Sales are concentrated in specific geographic regions.
A few high-value customers significantly impact total sales.
Higher-priced products can generate strong revenue depending on demand.
Sales performance across employees is relatively balanced.

Business Recommendations:
Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue
Strengthen relationships with top customers.
Expand operations in high-performing regions.
Optimize pricing strategies for better profitability.

Learning Outcomes:
Built end-to-end data visualization pipeline.
Strengthened data cleaning and aggregation understanding.
Improved Tableau dashboard design and storytelling.
Translated data into actionable business insights.
